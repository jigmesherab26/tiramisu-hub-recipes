Classic Italian Tiramisu

★★★★★ 4.8

Servings: 6 | Prep Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese: 250 g
  • Egg yolks: 4
  • Granulated sugar: 100 g
  • Ladyfinger biscuits: 200 g
  • Espresso coffee: 250 ml
  • Cocoa powder: for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale and creamy.
  2. Fold mascarpone gently into the egg mixture.
  3. Dip ladyfingers briefly into espresso.
  4. Layer biscuits and cream alternately in a dish.
  5. Chill for at least 4 hours.
  6. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Notes

For best results, prepare the tiramisu a day ahead to allow flavors to fully develop. Use high-quality espresso and cocoa.
