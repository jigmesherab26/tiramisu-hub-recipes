Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Egg yolks
  • Fine sugar
  • Ladyfingers
  • Espresso coffee
  • Unsweetened cocoa

Instructions

  1. Brew espresso and allow to cool.
  2. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale.
  3. Fold mascarpone gently.
  4. Layer soaked ladyfingers and cream.
  5. Finish with cocoa dusting.
